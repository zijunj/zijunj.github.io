Assignment 8

In this assignment, I create a web application that experimented with Logistic regression clusters. I learned that As the shift distance between clusters increases, the model’s intercept and coefficients adjust to better center and orient the decision boundary for optimal class separation. The logistic loss decreases with greater cluster separation, as the task becomes easier, while the margin width grows, reflecting clearer distinctions between classes.
